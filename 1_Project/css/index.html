NewJeans

Everything about newjeans

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of Hybe Corporation.
The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They debuted with the single "Attention" in July 2022 and released their debut extended play, "New Jeans," in August 2022. NewJeans has gained significant recognition in the music industry, achieving milestones such as selling 4.39 million album copies in a year, breaking records on Spotify, and receiving accolades like Artist of the Year at various award shows. Additionally, the group has made philanthropic contributions and has been praised for their influence on the fashion industry.
